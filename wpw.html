About The Workshop

"Skill in photography is acquired by practice and not by purchase "

- Percy W. Harris

Shutterbugs Photography Club, MITAOE presents an online photography workshop for 6 days, starting from 13th August 2021, for everyone who have been finding an opportunity to learn photography and post editing. Participants will be given e-certificate after the completion of workshop through emails only if they attend all 6 Days sessions.

  • Fundamentals of Photography
  • Mobile Photography
  • Framing and Composition
  • Various Genres of Photography
  • Location Online
  • Date & Time 5 PM - 7 PM, 13 th Aug 2021 - 18 thAug 2021
  • Speakers 10 Experts

5 PM - 7 PM Workshop

Basics of Photography

Rutwik Deshmukh

This is an important session to brush up and get a good hold on the concepts for amature photographers. Fundamental topics related to photography i.e. exposure triangle ( ISO, Shutter Speed and Aperture) will be covered.

5 PM - 6 PM Workshop

Mobile Photography

Raghavendra Chate

It is a myth that we need gears to do good photography. Each one of us has a smartphone consisting of different numbers of lenses providing features like pro mode. This session will bring light on the topic of using a mobile phone’s camera to click amazing pictures by knowing the tips and tricks.

9.30 - 10.30 AM Workshop

Composition and Framing

Sanket Urane

Everyone can click pictures after getting an idea of the basics of photography. But to capture eye catching frames it’s mandatory to understand the most important topic of composition and framing. This session will be covering the common mistakes of framing along with tips for correcting the mistakes.

5 PM - 6 PM Workshop

Silhouettes & BackLight

Shobhit Goyal

One of the most interesting genres of photography is based on playing with sunlight as well as artificial light. This session will elaborate on the genre along with the detailings regarding the approach of clicking silhouettes.

6 PM - 7 PM Workshop

Portrait

Yukta Amrao

Portrait is the soul of photography that might sound interesting but needs lots of focus on the details required to do it. This session will include the introduction of portraits as well as the secret of good portrait photography will be revealed.

5 PM - 6 PM Workshop

Minimal

Aditya Chavan

The Minimal sounds very simple according to its definition. But to acquire the right skills for minimal photography requires lots of imagination and creativity which will be shared in the session along with the correct techniques.

6 PM - 7 PM Workshop

Astro/ LEP/ Steel Wool

Chetan Patil

LEP (Long Exposure photography) is one of the genres that creates striking and stunning photographs . Mastering it requires a big checklist of clearing understanding of the exposure triangle as well as good hold on the camera which will be taught in depth in the session.

5 PM - 6 PM Workshop

Flat Lay/ Product

Mayank Patel

The most demanding genre in terms of commercial market is product photography including the flatlay. The arrangement and detailing of the subject requires attention to capture appealing photographs. This session will include all important points covering the mistakes made by the amateur photographers as well.

6 PM - 7 PM Workshop

Macro

Sunil Kumbar

The art of making tiny things look big might sound easy but gets intricating when doing macro photography. The session will cover all the essential conditions and requirements taking into consideration mobile and camera photography.

5 PM - 6 PM Workshop

Flat Lay/ Product

Nil

The most demanding genre in terms of commercial market is product photography including the flatlay. The arrangement and detailing of the subject requires attention to capture appealing photographs. This session will include all important points covering the mistakes made by the amateur photographers as well.

6 PM - 7 PM Workshop

Color Grading & Reels

Subhankar Panda

The current trend of getting good reach for each instagram user is reels. Though creating reels sounds like a cakewalk, it actually involves lots of creativity along with following the right steps to create viral reels will be explained in the session along with colour grading .
